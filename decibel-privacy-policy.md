Privacy Policy — Decibel by MadPocket
  
  Privacy Policy
  Last updated: June 19, 2026

  MadPocket ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Decibel mobile application
  (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection,
  use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

  1. MICROPHONE USAGE
  
  Decibel uses your device microphone solely to measure ambient sound levels
  in real time. No audio is ever recorded, stored on the device, or
  transmitted to any server. Microphone access is used exclusively for
  live dB SPL calculations displayed on screen.

  2. DATA WE COLLECT

  We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly.
  The App does not require account registration or login.

  However, we use third-party services that may collect certain data
  automatically:
  
  a) Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC)
     Collects crash reports when the App unexpectedly closes. Reports may
     include device model, operating system version, App version, and
     anonymized stack traces. This data is used solely to identify and
     fix bugs.

  b) Firebase Analytics (Google LLC)
     Collects anonymized usage statistics such as which features are used,
     session duration, and device type. This helps us understand how the
     App is used and improve future versions.

  c) Firebase Performance Monitoring (Google LLC)
     Collects anonymized performance data such as App startup time and
     response times. This data is used to optimize App performance.

  All Firebase services are operated by Google LLC. Data collected by
  Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy:
  https://policies.google.com/privacy

  3. DATA STORAGE AND RETENTION
  
  Measurement session data (dB readings, timestamps, custom session names)
  is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.
  You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App.

  Crash, analytics, and performance data collected by Firebase is retained
  according to Google's data retention policies (typically 90 days for
  Crashlytics, 14 months for Analytics).

  4. LEGAL BASIS (GDPR)
  
  If you are located in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for
  collecting data via Firebase is our legitimate interest in maintaining
  App stability and improving user experience (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).

  5. YOUR RIGHTS

  Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:
  • Access the data collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

  To exercise these rights, contact us at: hello@madpocket.app

  6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

  The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not
  knowingly collect data from children.

  7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
  
  We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be
  posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App
  after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
